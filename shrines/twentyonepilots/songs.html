holding on to you

holding on to you was the first song i knew that would be a favorite. not even just the lyrics, but also the sound of the song just scratches my brain perfectly! to me the song is about taking control of your mental health and clinging onto whatever you can to stay alive. for me, my friends (and music <3) are the ones i'm holding on to. my friends never fail to keep me going, even through my worst moments.

lovely

i love this song so so so so much omg. along with the instrumentals, i love a lyrics a lot. the chorus lyric is "won't you stay alive, i'll take you on a ride. i will make you believe you are lovely!" to me those lyrics always spoke to me in a certain way. it has always made me feel more connected to the band, knowing that those lyrics aren't for a singular person, but also for the fans.
