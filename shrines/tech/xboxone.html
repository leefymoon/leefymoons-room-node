I LOVE TECH

the xbox one s(hit edition)

this console was with me since 2017. this thing got me through all of the hard times up until it was the hard time. i got it for xmas in 2017 and i LOVED it. i played minecraft and roblox nearly everyday with my friend. as i grew old i played less and less, but never abandoned gaming completely.

fast forward to june-ish 2025, the xbox starts acting funny. immediately i knew, this thing is finally on its death bed. now by this point i was basically over console gaming and i had started gaming on my father's pc. like i predicted, the xbox started slowly bootting up more and more, until the point to when it would boot up it wouldn't even load the screen for my account pin. it even managed to corrupt some part of the xbox and i had to factory reset it! this is when i knew i had to let it go. so i said my goodbyes and swore to never use it again. then shortly after my father gave me his pc, so thank you for your sacrifice xbox.
