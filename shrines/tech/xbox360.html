the poverty 360

oh my sweet boy. this xbox is an xbox 360 E. which was the 360 released alongside the xbox one... it also has a winchester mobo, meaning it cannot be hardmodded. how sad. oh but how i LOVED this thing... that is until it started overheating anytime i tried using it. so it sat unused for YEARS until 2025 when i decided to take it apart with my father. we changed the thermal paste and thought all would be fine and dandy. but! of course not. it still overheat so i gave up, yet again.

that leads us into january 2026. i was chatting with my friends as one does and somehow the xbox 360 came up. i remembered that my xbox 360 was just sitting collecting dust. i decided to just bite the bullet and open it up again! what do i discover? nothing. it seemed fine! new thermal paste and everything! that was until i turned it one without its case. its fan wasn't working! after all these years i had thought the fan was completely out of the question, but nope! so i ordered a new fan and all worked just fine!
