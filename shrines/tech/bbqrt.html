the BBQRT

ah yes my surface rt, this bastard is OLDDDD. ok now maybe not that old... i believe my mother got this thing in 2013 or so just to have a computer. and BOY was it one of the worst options. i mean we're talking arm32 infra, a nvidia tegra 3, and like 2 gb of ram. sounds awful right? i hate it! all i remeber from this thing is that i LOVED playing games on it. i also loved snooping in the settings, how else do you think i became a nerd?

back in october 2025 or so, i decided "hey, i have that surface rt, i should see if i can mod it in any way." and to not much surprise at all, my friend managed to find a raspiOS image exactly for the surface rt. so of course now it has raspiOS on it and i have yet to yet any actual good use for it. you also may be wondering why it's called the BBQRT, well my friend it's a short story really. as i went to take out the flash drive with the image of raspiOS on it, i managed to drop it into my bbq sauce for my chicken nuggs. i told my friend about this and his immediate thought was, "omg it's the bbqrt now". so yeah :kek:
